=== GENERAL FEEDING HABITS & HYGIENE ===
- Environment: Designated place, minimum distraction (No TV/Mobile).
- Engagement: Loving eye contact, storytelling, naming colors/shapes.
- Hygiene: Wash hands before feeding, sit in one place.
- Self-feeding: Let the baby handle food, spoons, and cups to learn.
- Family Meals: Eat together whenever possible.
- Variety: Change taste, consistency, and cooking methods (coconut milk, tempered, mellum) to avoid food refusal.

=== 6 MONTHS (INTRODUCTION) ===
- Goal: Complement breast milk, not replace it.
- Days 1-3: Semi-solid soft well-mashed rice mixed with breast milk. 2-3 teaspoons.
- Days 4-9: Add pulses (dhal). Add animal origin food (powdered sprats, fish, chicken) for iron. Use thick coconut milk/oil/butter.
- Days 10-30: Add vegetables (pumpkin, carrot, sweet potato) and green leaves (gotukola).
- Texture: Very smooth, semi-solid, well-mashed.
- Frequency: 2-3 times per day.
- Portion: Start with 2-3 teaspoons, increasing to 1/4 tea cup.
- Prohibited: No salt, No sugar, No honey, No raw egg.

=== 7-8 MONTHS (TRANSITION) ===
- Foods to introduce: Jak, bread fruit, finger foods (ripe avocado, soft center of hopper, bread with butter).
- Texture: Gradually increase consistency. By 8 months, use coarsely chopped texture.
- Frequency: 2-3 times per day.
- Portion: 1/2 tea cup to bit more than 1/2 tea cup.
- Fruits: 2-3 teaspoons, 1-2 times a day.
- Fluids: Sips of boiled cooled water after meals.
- Prohibited: No salt, No sugar, No honey, No raw egg. Never leave baby alone with finger foods (choking risk).

=== 9-11 MONTHS (ESTABLISHING) ===
- Main meals: Coarsely chopped family foods.
- Snacks: 1-2 nutritious snacks (boiled egg, boiled potato with coconut/butter).
- Frequency: 3-4 times per day.
- Portion: Bit more than 1/2 tea cup.
- Prohibited: No salt, No sugar, No honey.

=== 12-24 MONTHS (FAMILY FOODS) ===
- Main meals: Nutritious family foods.
- Variety: 5 different items of vegetables and fruits per day.
- Frequency: 3 times per day + 1-2 nutritious snacks.
- Portion: Bit more than 1 tea cup per meal.
- Daily Requirements:
    * Grains (Rice/String hoppers): 2 cups
    * Pulses (Dhal/Green gram): 3-6 tbsp
    * Vegetables: 3-6 tbsp
    * Green Leaves: 1/2 tea cup
    * Sprats: 6-8 numbers
    * Meat/Fish/Liver: Two match-box-size pieces
    * Kunisso: 2 tablespoons
    * Egg: 1 per day
    * Fruits: Twice a day
    * Dairy: If no breast milk, 1 cup curd/yoghurt or match-box-size cheese.

=== ILLNESS & RECOVERY ===
- During Illness: Soft, easily digestible food in small amounts frequently. Add extra coconut milk.
- After Recovery: Give one extra meal a day for two weeks to catch up on growth.
